About Me

I'm Ananya N, a cybersecurity enthusiast from Bengaluru with a passion for finding vulnerabilities before the bad guys do. I specialise in web and mobile application security, with hands-on experience in penetration testing across real-world SaaS and mobile platforms.

I've worked on everything from SQL injection and XSS to certificate pinning bypasses and IDOR on Android apps. I document every finding with industry-standard CVSS scoring and OWASP mappings — because a good pentest isn't just about finding bugs, it's about communicating them clearly.

When I'm not hunting for vulnerabilities, you'll find me on HackTheBox and TryHackMe, sharpening my skills one challenge at a time — or out feeding the 20 stray dogs in my neighbourhood, because protecting the vulnerable applies offline too.
